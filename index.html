Irrigation Systems Simulator

Travelling Irrigator

A travelling irrigator moves across the field in a straight line, distributing water as it travels. It's commonly used for rectangular fields and can cover large areas efficiently.

Lateral Irrigator

Lateral irrigation systems consist of a series of sprinklers mounted on a pipeline that moves in a straight line across a field. They're ideal for rectangular fields and provide uniform water distribution.

Center Pivot Irrigator

Center pivot irrigation systems rotate around a central pivot point, creating circular irrigation patterns. They're widely used for their efficiency and ability to cover large circular areas with minimal labor.
